8 ## Resolution cascade (per CID)

10 1. **ToUnicode CMap** — if the font has a `/ToUnicode` stream, its `bf_char`/

16 `{idx, "cid:0xHHHH"}` appended to the unresolved list.
17
18 ## `__test_cmap__` shortcut
19
20 For unit tests, a pre-parsed `%Pdf.Reader.CMap{}` can be injected by storing
21 it in the font dict under the key `"__test_cmap__"`. This bypasses stream
22 resolution. (Mirrors the same shortcut in `Pdf.Reader.Font`.)
23
24 ## Width / advance computation
25
26 This module handles **character decoding only** (bytes → Unicode text). Glyph
27 advance widths (`/W` and `/DW` entries on the DescendantFonts[0] dict) are read
28 separately by `Pdf.Reader.Font.Widths` (§ 9.7.4.3). The two concerns are
29 intentionally kept in separate modules: decoding and advance computation are
30 independent of each other.
31
