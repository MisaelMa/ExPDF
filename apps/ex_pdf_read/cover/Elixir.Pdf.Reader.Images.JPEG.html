22 | 0xC3 | SOF3 | Lossless (sequential) |
23 | 0xC5 | SOF5 | Differential sequential DCT |
24 | 0xC6 | SOF6 | Differential progressive DCT |

30 | 0xCE | SOF14 | Differential progressive DCT (arithmetic) |

33 Non-SOF markers to skip:
34 | 0xC4 | DHT | Huffman table |
35 | 0xC8 | JPG | JPEG extensions |

39
40 0xFF 0xCy — marker (2 bytes)
