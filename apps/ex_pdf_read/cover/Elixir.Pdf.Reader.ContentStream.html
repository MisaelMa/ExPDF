62 ## Position math
63
64 Absolute position of a glyph run start:
65
66 M_render = Tm × CTM
67 x = M_render.e, y = M_render.f
68
69 Spec: PDF 1.7 § 8.3.3 (row-vector convention), § 9.4.4 (text advance).
70
71 ## Glyph advance (§ 9.4.4)
72
73 Text-matrix advance uses the full PDF § 9.4.4 formula per glyph:
74
75 tx = ((w/1000 - Tj_kern) * Tfs + Tc + Tw_if_space) * Th
76
77 Where `w` comes from the active font's `widths_fn` closure (set by `Tf`).
78 Fonts without embedded `/Widths` produce `w=0`; advance is then driven
79 only by `Tc`/`Tw`/`Tj_kern` (documented gap for Standard-14 fonts).
80
81 Position of the START of each run is exact (derived from Tm at call time).
82
83 ## Unknown operator strategy
84
85 When an unrecognized operator is encountered, the entire operand stack is cleared
86 (per PDF spec § 7.8.2 which states conforming readers should process what they can).
87 This is the same strategy used by most major PDF parsers (e.g. pdfminer-six, pdf.js).
